About Me

Hi I'm Vincent, a sophomore at Tufts University. I'm currently majoring in Computer Science, and I have a strong ambition to make an impact on the web and my communities. I discovered my passion for programming in high school and since then, I've taught myself new languages and frameworks using online resources such as Udacity, Udemy, YouTube, and online blogs. I'm currently learning Web Development and Android App Development, and I hope to bring my ideas to life in the near future. When I'm not busy, I enjoy playing around, jumping into outdoor adventures and experimenting with photography.

Research Intern

He also developed natural human interface that controlled NAO's upper body motions with human gestures using the XBox Kinect Sensor.
